Kryptos

This page contains the pseudo Vigenere cipher used by Kyrptos. For the regular Vigenère cipher, see this page.



Kryptos parts 1 and 2:

Kryptos uses two keys in its cipher. One is the standard Vigenère key, while the other is used to re-order the alphabet. Leaving key 2 blank will use the standard Vigenere chipher.
